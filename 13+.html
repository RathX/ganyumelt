Common questions

  • What’s the Cash Card?

    The Cash Card is our Visa debit card, issued by Cash's partner banks. You can use it to pay online and in-store from your Cash App balance. Once you activate your card, you can add it to Apple Pay or Google Pay, even before the physical card arrives in the mail.

  • How can I keep my account safe?

    You can reinforce your account security with these steps:

    • Set up a PIN and keep it secret.
    • Turn on notifications to monitor account activity.
    • Restrict money requests to contacts only.

    Remember, Cash App will never ask for your PIN, and will only communicate through the app or verified accounts. Learn more about keeping your money secure.

  • What controls do parents / guardians have over the account?

    Parents and guardians will be able to see a full record of transactions and transfers in their monthly statements. They can also deactivate the Cash Card and account at any time by contacting support.
